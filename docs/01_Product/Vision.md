# Vision and Strategic Direction

## 1. Product Vision
To transform classrooms into intelligent, interactive, and personalized learning environments.

## 2. Mission Statement
To empower educators and learners through trustworthy AI, personalized guidance, and intelligent automation that extends learning beyond the classroom.

## 3. Core Motto & Tagline
* **Motto:** No Question Left Behind.
* **Tagline:** Beyond Scores. Towards Understanding.

## 4. Key Pillars
1. **Trustworthy AI:** Grounding AI assistants in lecturer-approved materials to prevent hallucinations.
2. **Personalized Guidance:** Adapting to individual student learning paces and tracking verified achievements.
3. **Intelligent Automation:** Eliminating repetitive administrative workloads for educators using workflow automation (UiPath).
