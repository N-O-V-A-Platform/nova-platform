# Research & Background

## 1. Domain Overview
N.O.V.A. operates at the intersection of AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and EdTech. This document compiles research on educational psychology, the role of virtual assistants in student engagement, and methods for grounding LLMs in course materials.

## 2. Research Objectives
* Investigate cognitive load reduction for lecturers.
* Study the impact of interactive, low-friction QA tools on student classroom participation.
* Analyze optimal context retrieval techniques for pedagogical materials.
