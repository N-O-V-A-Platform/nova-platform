## 3. Competitive Advantages
* **High Trust:** RAG limits hallucinations, preventing incorrect academic tutoring.
* **Closed-loop Escalation:** Automatically routes unresolved questions directly to the teaching staff.
* **Low Administrative Overhead:** Automates grading prep, attendance assistance, and reporting.
